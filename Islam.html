Islam

Islam is an Abrahamic monotheistic religion teaching that there is oly one god Allah, and that Muhammad is the messanger of god. It is the worlds second largest religion and the fastest growing major religion in the world, with over 1.8 billion followers or 24.1 % of the global population, known as muslams. Islam teaches that god is merciful, all - powerful, unique. It has guided mankind through prophets, revealed scriptures and natural signs.
